About

Who Are We

Gachie Mwanza & Co. Advocates is a premier provider of legal services in the areas of Debt Collection, Commercial Law, Conveyancing and Civil Litigation. With offices in Nairobi, Gachie Mwanza & Co. Advocates is a rapidly growing and innovative company committed to providing quality and personalized service to each of our clients.

Stephen Mwanza Gachie is the founder and Managing Partner of Gachie Mwanza & Co. Advocates and is responsible for the vision and direction of the law firm.

Having represented a variety of clients corporate among other individual clients, he has developed a wealth of experience in efficiently managing the large volume of information generated in complex reorganization and mass tort litigation cases. Among his major corporate clients are:

  • All Africa Conference of Churches (AACC)
  • Association of Evangelicals in Africa Trustees
  • Kenya Orient Insurance Company Limited
  • Milele Management and Milele Centre Limited
  • Hutchings Beimer Limited
  • Pago Airways and Travel Services Limited
  • Jakaranda Millers Limited
  • Kirima Bus Services Limited
  • Darasha Investments Limited
  • Church Commissioners of Kenya
  • Kentex Developers Limited
  • Arizonas Hotels Limited
  • Vanga Express Limited
  • Grand Holiday Hotels Limited
  • Sungreen International Company Limited of the United States and its local subsidiary, Sungreen Company Limited.
  • Juja City Mall Limited
  • Mountain Mall Limited

His industry experience is diverse and includes real estate and Housing, Health Care, Manufacturing, Banking and Insurance.

Before the partnership was founded he was employed in various law firms and corporate organizations where he gained diverse and immense experience in handling legal matters, which formed the basis of the coming together to form the firm.
