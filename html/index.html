Who Are We

Gachie Mwanza & Co. Advocates is a premier provider of legal services in the areas of Debt Collection, Commercial Law, Conveyancing and Civil Litigation. With offices in Nairobi, Gachie Mwanza & Co. Advocates is a rapidly growing and innovative company committed to providing quality and personalized service to each of our clients.

What We Do

Though we generally conduct all the legal matters which come our way, we have chosen to focus on our core competencies.

Currently, we are focused on services in three main areas which are of great concern in the modern commercial world.

  • Litigation Department
  • Conveyancing
  • Commercial Law Department

How We Work

We believe in a pro-active approach towards work; therefore, we tailor our processes to the needs of our clients. Through this, we are able to offer personalized services to our clients whenever called upon to do so.

To facilitate our operations and enhance quality service delivery at all times, we have the following standard operating procedures put in place:

  • File and defend cases which are brought to our attention in the Courts which have specific jurisdiction to deal with them.
  • Persist and follow up in all our matters whether they have been filed in courts or not with all reasonable speed.
  • Updating our clients on all the progress by Email, Mobile or otherwise.
  • Giving a legal opinion at the earliest possible moment to our clients in accordance with the particular brief.
  • Where we are handling several matters for a particular client we do periodic write-ups and/or status reports updating our clients on their respective files.
  • We have laid special emphasis on all matters in the Various Lands Offices across the country, Motor Vehicle Registry, as well as Sheria House as this is an area where delays, missing files, and lethargy towards work have been known to slow case procedures.
  • Where we have to execute Court Decrees we employ the services of well-tested and honest Auctioneers of high professional integrity to do the work and we have a variety among our panel.
